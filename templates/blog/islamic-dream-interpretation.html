Islamic Dream Interpretation

In Islamic tradition, dreams are considered an important form of spiritual insight. Many scholars studied dreams and their meanings.

One of the most famous dream interpreters was Ibn Sirin, whose writings about dreams are still studied today.

According to Islamic teachings, dreams can be divided into three types: true dreams from God, disturbing dreams from negative influences, and ordinary dreams from daily thoughts.

Symbols in dreams such as water, light, animals, or journeys are often interpreted based on religious and moral meanings.
