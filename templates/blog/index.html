Dream Interpretation in Ancient Egypt

Dream interpretation played an important role in ancient Egyptian culture. The ancient Egyptians believed that dreams were messages from the gods.

Priests and spiritual interpreters studied dreams carefully. Many temples had special dream interpreters who helped people understand their dreams.

Dream Books in Ancient Egypt

One of the most famous discoveries is the Egyptian Dream Book. It contains many dream symbols and their meanings.

For example:

  • Seeing water in a dream meant purification and renewal.
  • Dreaming about flying symbolized spiritual freedom.
  • Seeing snakes could represent danger or divine power.

Dreams as Messages

Egyptians believed that dreams could predict the future or provide guidance. People sometimes slept in temples hoping to receive a divine dream.

This practice is known as dream incubation and was common in many ancient cultures.
