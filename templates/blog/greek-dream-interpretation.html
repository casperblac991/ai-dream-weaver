Dream Interpretation in Ancient Greece

In ancient Greek culture, dreams were believed to be messages from the gods. People often visited temples seeking guidance through dreams.

One famous center for dream interpretation was the temple of Asclepius, where people practiced dream incubation to receive healing visions.

Greek philosophers and historians wrote about dreams and their meanings, believing that dreams could reveal hidden truths about the future and the human soul.

Symbols appearing in dreams such as animals, storms, and journeys were interpreted as signs related to destiny, health, and divine communication.
