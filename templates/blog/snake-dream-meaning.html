Snake Dream Meaning

Dreaming about snakes is one of the most common dream experiences reported throughout human history.

In many ancient cultures, snakes symbolized transformation, danger, healing, or hidden knowledge.

In some traditions, a snake in a dream represents a hidden enemy or a warning about betrayal. In other cultures, snakes symbolize rebirth and personal change.

The meaning of a snake dream often depends on the situation in the dream, the behavior of the snake, and the feelings experienced by the dreamer.

Understanding dream symbols can help people reflect on their emotions and subconscious thoughts.
