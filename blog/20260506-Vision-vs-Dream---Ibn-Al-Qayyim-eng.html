Vision vs. Dream - Ibn Al-Qayyim

📅 2026-05-06 ⏱️ 6 min read 🔖 1292-1350 CE 🆔 Article #20260506
Imam Ibn Al-Qayyim distinguishes three types: 🌟 **Vision (Ru'ya):** • From Allah • Good news or warning • Clear and true • Comes to righteous 💭 **Dream (Hulm):** • From Satan • Causes sadness • Confusing nightmares • Frightens the sleeper 🧠 **Self-Talk:** • From daily thoughts • Reflects concerns • No interpretation How to distinguish: - Vision: brings good news - Dream: causes fear - Self-talk: reflects reality
