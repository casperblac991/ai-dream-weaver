Dream of Water Interpretation - Symbols and Meanings

📅 2026-05-02 | 📂 تفسير أحلام | 👁️ 0 views

Water in dreams has multiple and important meanings:

  • Clear Water: Symbolizes purity, clarity, and inner peace.
  • Murky Water: May indicate confusion, anxiety, and problems.
  • Flooding: May mean feeling overwhelmed and under severe pressure.
  • Swimming: Expresses the ability to deal with emotions and feelings.

Water in general is associated with emotions and inner feelings, so pay attention to the state of water in your dream.
