Asklepieia Temples - Healing Through Dreams

📅 2026-05-06 ⏱️ 7 min read 🔖 500 BCE - 400 CE 🆔 Article #20260506
In Ancient Greece, Asklepieia temples were built for healing through dreams. 🏛️ **Temple Description:** • Spread throughout Greece • Famous ones at Epidaurus and Kos • Had dormitories for healing sleep 💤 **Healing Process:** 1. Patient purified and fasted 2. Offered sacrifice to healing god 3. Slept in sacred dormitory 4. Received healing dream 5. Priests interpreted and prescribed 🌟 **Morpheus - God of Dreams:** • Appeared in human form • Could imitate any shape • Son of Hypnos (sleep god) 📚 **Philosophers on Dreams:** • Aristotle: wrote about dreams • Plato: dreams are soul windows • Hippocrates: dreams reveal illness
