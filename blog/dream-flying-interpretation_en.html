Dream of Flying Interpretation - What Does It Mean?

📅 2026-05-02 | 📂 تفسير أحلام | 👁️ 0 views

Flying dreams are among the most common and positive dreams. They usually symbolize:

  • Freedom and Liberation: Flying expresses your desire to break free from constraints and pressures.
  • Success and Ambition: It may indicate the achievement of your dreams and aspirations.
  • Self-Confidence: Flying with confidence means you believe in your abilities.
  • Escape from Problems: Sometimes it may mean your desire to escape from a problem.

If you fly easily and comfortably, it indicates your feelings of happiness and satisfaction. However, if you fall or fear falling, it may mean your fear of failure.
