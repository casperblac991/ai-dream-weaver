Imam Ibn Sirin - The Master Interpreter

📅 Jun 19, 2026 | 🏷️ Islamic Heritage
Imam Muhammad Ibn Sirin (654-728 CE) is the most famous dream interpreter in Islamic history. His methodology: • Never interprets without prayer • Asks about the dreamer's state • Based on Quran and Sunnah Famous interpretations: • Seeing Prophet Muhammad is true • Sea represents king or knowledge • Snake represents enemy • Flying represents travel or death
