Animal Dreams Interpretation - Complete Guide

📅 2026-05-02 | 📂 تفسير أحلام | 👁️ 0 views

Animals in dreams have special symbols:

  • Lion: Symbolizes strength, courage, and authority.
  • Snake: May indicate danger or wisdom depending on context.
  • Bird: Expresses freedom, hope, and aspiration.
  • Dog: Symbolizes loyalty, protection, and friendship.
  • Cat: May indicate independence and mystery.

Remember that the meaning of an animal depends on its behavior and your interaction with it in the dream.
